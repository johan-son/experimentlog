Conclusion, or; What's Next?

All the experiments I’ve done have centred around digital “making” as a sort of response to phenomena in our “real” world.

At the moment, the next steps would be to look deeper into the relationship between digital “making” and analogue “making”, and to consider what the relationship between the digital “space” and the analogue “space” might mean for worldbuilding.

03 / Conclusion, or; What's Next?

As a result of all my experiments and contextual anchor research, it looks like my work is headed towards something more graphic novel-ly/webcomic-y — especially revolving around themes of an afterlife and how a playful interpretation of what happens after death might help destigmatise conversations about loss.

At the moment, the next steps would be to look deeper into the relationship between digital “making” and analogue “making”, and to consider what the relationship between the digital “space” and the analogue “space” might mean for worldbuilding.
